How to Make Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (around 1.5kg)
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-inch ginger, sliced
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Spring onions (for garnish)
  • Soy sauce and chili sauce (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of water with salt and ginger.
  2. Add the whole chicken and simmer for 30–40 minutes until cooked.
  3. Remove the chicken, brush with sesame oil, and set aside.
  4. Rinse rice, then sauté with garlic and ginger in a pot or rice cooker.
  5. Add chicken broth (from boiling the chicken) to cook the rice.
  6. Slice the chicken and serve over the fragrant rice.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and serve with soy and chili sauce.

Tips

  • Use the leftover broth to make a light soup to serve on the side.
  • Chill the chicken in ice water for firmer skin and juicier meat.
